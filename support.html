Frequently Asked Questions

How do I subscribe to Premium?

1. Open the app and tap "Subscribe" on the home screen
2. Choose between Monthly ($4.99/month) or Yearly ($29.99/year - save 50%)
3. Tap "Subscribe" and complete the purchase with your Apple ID
4. Your premium features will be activated immediately

What are the Premium features?

Premium subscribers get:
• 3 on-demand vibes per day (tap "Send vibes" button)
• Daily scheduled encouragement notifications
• All 4 tone options (Warm, Energetic, Calm, Witty)
• Unlimited favorites
• Priority support

What is the First Day Bonus?

New users get 3 free on-demand vibes on their first day to try out the app! After 24 hours, you'll need a Premium subscription to continue using on-demand vibes.

How do I restore my subscription?

If you reinstalled the app or switched devices:
1. Open the app and go to Settings
2. Tap "Restore Purchases"
3. Your subscription will be restored automatically

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID:
1. Open Settings on your iPhone/iPad
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Select "Daily Vibeshift"
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

I'm not receiving notifications

Make sure notifications are enabled:
1. Open Settings on your device
2. Scroll down and tap "Daily Vibeshift"
3. Tap "Notifications"
4. Enable "Allow Notifications"
5. Make sure "Scheduled Delivery" is turned off

How do I change my vibe tone?

On the home screen, tap one of the tone buttons:
• Warm - Compassionate and supportive
• Energetic - Upbeat and motivating
• Calm - Peaceful and reassuring
• Witty - Clever and humorous

The app crashed or isn't working

Try these steps:
1. Force close the app and reopen it
2. Restart your device
3. Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
4. If the issue persists, contact us at rigenm@gmail.com

How do I delete my data?

All your data is stored locally on your device. To delete it:
1. Go to Settings tab in the app
2. Tap "Clear All Favorites" to remove saved vibes
3. Or delete the app to remove all data
