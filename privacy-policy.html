Privacy Policy for Daily Vibeshift

Last Updated: November 5, 2025

Simple Summary: Daily Vibeshift does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Daily Vibeshift is designed with your privacy in mind. All data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

2. Data Storage

The following information is stored locally on your device only:

  • Your encouragement messages (vibes)
  • Your favorite messages
  • Your tone preferences (Warm, Energetic, Calm, or Witty)
  • Your notification settings
  • Your subscription status (managed by Apple)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or anyone else.

3. Message Generation

When you request a new encouragement message, your selected tone preference is sent anonymously to our server to generate a personalized message. We do not:

  • Store any information about who made the request
  • Track your usage patterns
  • Collect any identifying information
  • Link requests to any user account

4. In-App Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs how your purchase information is handled.

5. Push Notifications

If you enable notifications, Apple's Push Notification Service is used to deliver your daily encouragement messages. We do not track whether you open or interact with notifications.

6. Third-Party Services

Daily Vibeshift does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

8. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear All Favorites" option in the app settings
  • Deleting the app from your device

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

Email: rigenm@gmail.com

11. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data for us to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over all app data stored on your device.
